US citizens are often eligible to vote while abroad. More information can be found on the
[State Department website](https://travel.state.gov/en/international-travel/living-abroad/voting.html)
and the [Federal Voting Assistance Program website](https://www.fvap.gov/citizen-voter/overview).
You may also find the [Overseas Vote initiative](https://www.overseasvotefoundation.org) helpful.
